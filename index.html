    The Versa Connect takes these attributes and focused on extending them seamlessly into the wheelchair accessible ascpects of out vehicle. It is our aim to product a coversion that is both practical and stylish, with an attention to detail that provides the same high quality look and feel throughout the vehicle.

    The Versa Connect is available in two wheelbases. The Short Versa Connect can accommodate the wheelchair user and up to 3 seated passengers (including the driver) or up to 5 seated passengers without the wheelchair. The Long Versa Connect can accommodate the wheelchair user and up to 5 seated passengers (including the driver) or up to 7 seated passengers without the wheelchair.

    Twin sliding doors alliw for ease of passengers access with a lightweight manual ramp to the rear for wheelchair access. As an optional extra, the ramp can have the added ability, using a simple lever, to fold at into the vehicle so that when the wheelchair user is not travelling, a huge and easily accessible boot space is created making the Versa Connect ever more practical
